Introduction

Kabutey ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our event management and social networking mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, username, profile picture
  • Authentication data (Google OAuth tokens)
  • Profile details such as bio and interests

Device & Usage Information

  • Device type, OS version, app version
  • Usage analytics and crash reports
  • Approximate location (if enabled)

Permissions We Use

Camera (android.permission.CAMERA)

  • Profile and event photo capture
  • QR code scanning for event check-ins
  • Photo sharing during events
Photos are accessed only when you initiate a camera action and are not uploaded without your consent.

Storage & Media Access

  • Selecting images from your gallery
  • Saving profile and event photos
  • Offline caching of event data

Location

  • Discovering nearby events
  • Venue-based check-ins
Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time.

Notifications

  • Event reminders and updates
  • Social activity alerts

How We Use Your Information

  • Event creation and discovery
  • Social networking features
  • Personalized recommendations
  • App security and fraud prevention
  • Improving app performance

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

  • Google services for authentication
  • Cloud infrastructure for secure storage
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Data Retention

  • Account data while your account is active
  • Deleted content is removed from our servers
  • Analytics data is anonymized

Your Rights

  • Access and update your information
  • Revoke permissions at device level
  • Delete your account and data
  • Request a copy of your data

Children’s Privacy

Kabutey is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.
