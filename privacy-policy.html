Privacy Policy for Kabutey

Effective Date: January 18, 2026
Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Introduction

Kabutey ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our event management and social networking mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, profile picture
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials, OAuth tokens from third-party services (Google)
  • Profile Data: Bio, interests, social connections within the app

Device and Usage Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Analytics: App interactions, feature usage, crash reports
  • Location Data: Approximate location for event discovery (when permitted)

Permissions and How We Use Them

Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)

We request camera access to:

  • Take profile photos and event photos
  • Scan QR codes for event check-ins and networking
  • Share photos during events

Note: Photos are stored locally on your device and only uploaded when you explicitly choose to share them.

Storage Permissions (READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES)

We request storage access to:

  • Save profile pictures and event photos
  • Access photos from your gallery for profile and event sharing
  • Cache event data for offline viewing

Location Permissions (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)

We request location access to:

  • Discover nearby events
  • Provide location-based event recommendations
  • Enable check-ins at event venues

Note: Location sharing is optional and can be disabled in app settings.

Notification Permissions (POST_NOTIFICATIONS, VIBRATE)

We request notification access to:

  • Send event reminders and updates
  • Notify you of new followers, likes, and comments
  • Alert you about relevant events and activities

Internet Access (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)

Required for:

  • Syncing event data and user profiles
  • Connecting with our servers
  • Loading event images and content
  • Real-time messaging and social features

How We Use Your Information

  • Event Management: Creating, discovering, and managing events
  • Social Features: Connecting with other users, sharing content
  • Personalization: Providing personalized event recommendations
  • Communication: Sending important updates and notifications
  • Security: Protecting your account and preventing fraud
  • Analytics: Improving app performance and user experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

Third-Party Services

  • Google Services: For authentication and analytics
  • Cloud Storage: For secure data backup and sync
  • Payment Processors: For event ticketing (if applicable)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized after 2 years
  • Photos and Content: Deleted when you remove them from the app
  • Cached Data: Cleared periodically for app performance

Your Rights and Choices

Account Control

  • Access: View your personal information in app settings
  • Update: Modify your profile and preferences anytime
  • Delete: Remove your account and associated data

Privacy Controls

  • Location: Disable location services in device settings
  • Notifications: Manage notification preferences in app
  • Camera/Photos: Revoke permissions in device settings
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data by contacting us

Children's Privacy

Kabutey is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through appropriate safeguards and compliance with applicable laws.

Changes to This Policy

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications
  • Updated effective date at the top of this policy
